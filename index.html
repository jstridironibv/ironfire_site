Who We Are

Built on Iron.
Forged in Fire.

IronFire Technologies is the software and platform arm of IronBridge Holdings — a multi-sector holding company grounded in IronWill, IronFaith, and IronService. We build purpose-driven applications designed from the ground up to serve people, not just metrics.

Technology without accountability is just code. We measure success not only in users and engagement, but in lives touched, research funded, and communities strengthened. Every product we ship is built with that standard in mind.

Our flagship platform, Enferno, is built in direct partnership with IronLimb Foundation — connecting athletes, tracking fitness impact, and translating community activity into real-world contributions to phantom limb syndrome research and veteran support.

IronFire Technologies

Enferno App

Enferno is IronFire's flagship AI-powered fitness and wellness platform — built for athletes who train with purpose. More than a training app, Enferno is designed to serve as the digital backbone of IronLimb Foundation, connecting its community, tracking athletic impact, and translating every rep into measurable real-world change.

Every workout logged. Every mile tracked. Every subscription tied to something bigger than the individual — phantom limb syndrome research and support for amputee veterans who deserve better.

01
AI Training Engine

Adaptive workout generation powered by AI. Programs that evolve with your performance, including specialized adaptive programs built for amputee athletes.

02
Impact Dashboard

See exactly how your training translates to dollars raised for phantom limb research and veteran support. Every workout has a value here — watch it grow.

03
Community Feed

A purpose-built social layer connecting athletes, veterans, and IronLimb Foundation advocates. A community that means something.

04
Mission-Linked Subscriptions

A direct portion of every Enferno subscription flows to IronLimb Foundation's research and support programs. Technology with purpose.
